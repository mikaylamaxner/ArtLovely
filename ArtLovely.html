The artist

My name is MiKayla Maxner. I grew up in Dickinson, North Dakota and Rapid City, South Dakota. Art has always been a natural talent of mine; specifically, drawing is where I excelled over the years. All of my portraits are made with graphite and charcoal; with this medium I focus and take pride in making my drawings look as realistic as possible. Using faces as my subject allows me to reach that goal while showing my full potential. I'm currently using this skill to become a freelance sketch artist. If you're interested in a portrait of your own, you can send me a drawing request through my Contact page.
